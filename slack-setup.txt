SLACK BOT SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

1. Open api.slack.com/apps in your browser
2. Click "Create New App"
3. Choose "From scratch"
4. Enter app name: Guac
5. Select your Slack workspace from the dropdown
6. Click "Create App"
7. On the left sidebar, click "OAuth & Permissions"
8. Scroll down to the "Bot Token Scopes" section
9. Click "Add an OAuth Scope"
10. Type "chat:write" and select it
11. Click "Add an OAuth Scope" again
12. Type "users:read" and select it
13. Click "Add an OAuth Scope" again
14. Type "users:read.email" and select it
15. Scroll back up to the top of the page
16. Click the green "Install to Workspace" button
17. Click "Allow" on the authorization screen
18. You will see "Bot User OAuth Token" starting with xoxb-
19. Click "Copy" next to that token
20. Paste it into your .env file replacing the placeholder
